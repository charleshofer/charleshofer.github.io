Press Kit

Ignorant, knuckle-dragging death metal from deep in the bowels of Texas. New to the heavy music scene, Death File Red has opened for local favorites Deocculted and Manifest Insanity, and playing the 2021 Halloween Hell Fest at Come and Take It Live. They are currently recording their first release expected in early 2022.

Death File Red is a available for booking and loves doing last minute fill-ins, beer premiers, and benefits for convicts.
