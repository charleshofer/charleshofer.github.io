Press Kit

"A quintet of world-strong death metal. They don't even have their profile at Metal-Archives yet, but the sound quality of the guys surprised positively... With a lot of grit and weight, they rightly call themselves an ignorant death metal band." - Old Coffin Spirit

Knuckle-dragging death metal quintet from Austin, Texas. Fairly new to the scene, they have opened for giants Mortiferum, Imperial Triumphant, and Immolation, and have shared the stage with local favorites Bruka, Cathexis, and Deocculted. With one tour under their belt and a demo in the works, they love taking last minute shows and beer premiers.
