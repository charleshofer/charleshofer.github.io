Ignorant, knuckle-dragging death metal from Austin, Texas, Death File Red are up-and-commers in the Texas death metal scene. Playing the Oblivion Access and Wrecking Ball metal festivals, as well as gigging consistently around the state, their debut EP, Inhumanitarian is slated to release on October 6th, 2023.
